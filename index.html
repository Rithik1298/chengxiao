Cheng Xiao

Biography

Cheng Xiao (born July 15, 1998) is a talented Chinese singer, dancer, and actress. She was born in Shenzhen, Guangdong, China, and began her journey in the entertainment industry at a young age. Cheng Xiao made her debut as a member of the South Korean girl group WJSN in 2016. She later started her solo activities in China in early 2018 and took a hiatus from the group. After a five-year hiatus, she officially left WJSN in March 2023.

As an actress, Cheng Xiao is best known for her role as Tong Yao in the popular drama "Falling into Your Smile" (2021). She has also appeared in various TV series and reality shows, including "Legend of Awakening" and "Detective Chinatown". Cheng Xiao's solo music career took off with the release of her first single "If Love" in 2018, which debuted at No. 3 on QQ Music.

Cheng Xiao's journey is a testament to her dedication and passion for the arts, and she continues to captivate audiences with her talent and charm.
